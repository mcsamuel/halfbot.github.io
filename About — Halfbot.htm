About

Halfbot is an independent game developer founded in 2010 by Derek Laufman and Melvin Samuel and is based out of London, ON. Our team draws upon years of experience to bring innovative and commercially successful products to market. Halfbot is committed to deliver an enjoyable and compelling end user experience.

If you would like to get in touch with Halfbot directly, please go to the contact page on this site.
