July 12, 2011

Blocks Cometh Updated to v1.5

July 12, 2011/ Halfbot-Melvin

The Blocks Cometh Update is now live.

Halfbot is proud to announce it's much anticipated update to The Blocks Cometh.  Wanting to bring a deeper experience and satisfy our fans we took all of the suggestions and feedback and did our best to apply it all in one huge update.

New Features:

Landscape: The first thing we wanted to address was the fan response about wanting to play the game in Landscape. This was a big challenge and involved a lot of work. But in the end we were able to solve this issue in a way that should accomodate all players. Among that change we've added the following.

Casual & Arcade modes: These two modes introduce a life system that helps extend Gameplay and bring a new dimension to the game. Casual mode offers players a less aggressive experience, while the hardcore fans will get challenged to new heights in Arcade mode.

4 New Characters to Unlock.

Now with more then 30 achievements.

Bug fixes and optimization.

Introducing In App Purchases: It's no surprise that it is a challenge being a small, self funded indie developer. In app purchases helps to fund development and allows us more time to work on our own IP's, bring you updates and continue to bring bigger and better games to the market place.

Gamebot Mode: Play The Blocks Cometh like it's 1989! This mode offers players a complete throw back to the old school handheld consoles. Complete with all new retro art, soundtrack and the custom Gamebot skin.

Unlock All Characters: Play with every character in the game instantly without having to unlock them through gameplay.

Grab it in the iTunes store here

Guest star... Mr. Destructoid!

January 31, 2011/ Derek Laufman

We are very proud to announce that the iOS release of The Blocks Cometh will feature non other than Destructoid.com's Mr.Destructoid!

Mr. Destructoid will be an unlockable character that will surely show those damn blocks a thing or two!!

We wanted to show the good folks at Destructoid that we were really grateful for everything they have done to help take down the copycat version of The Blocks Cometh and bring awareness to the stolen game situation that is causing chaos in the mobile world. So we thought how cool would it be if we had Mr. Destructoid make an appearance in the up coming iOS release? Well it's going to happen and we couldn't be happier!
